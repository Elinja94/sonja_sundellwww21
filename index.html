My stepmother's dogs

My stepmother has three Swedish vallhunds. They are very active dogs and are fun to play with. The oldest dog is called Pyry and he is very old and don't see or hear well anymore. The second oldest dog is Pilvi is mother of the youngest Tuisku. Swedish vallhunds are very old dog breed, they have actually old as vikings. Some people say they come from Sweden and others say they come from England. They are small dogs but very strong and very good shepherd dogs.
